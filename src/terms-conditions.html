1.6. AllowMeTo Limited shall be entitled to vary the Standard Charge/hourly rate with a 1-month notice period.

2.1. A confirmation accepting the terms and conditions is required before any work can commence.

2.2. A formal quote shall be provided outlining AllowMeTo Limited's hourly fee structure and any terms specific for an agreed project.

6.3. If you wish to cancel the arranged service, you can do so within the 14-day cooling-off period. During the 14 days, you can cancel for any reason and we will reimburse your money for all the unused hours. Your cooling-off period begins the day after you have accepted these terms and conditions.
